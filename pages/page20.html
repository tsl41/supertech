Alienware X15 R2 Specs

technuel Last Updated-April 15, 2023
Specification Value
Display 15.6-inch QHD (2560x1440), 240Hz
Processor Intel Core i7-12700H
RAM 32GB LPDDR5/5200MHz
Storage 1TB M.2 PCle NVMe SSD
Rear camera 1080p
Front camera 720p
Battery 87 Whr
Operating system Windows 11 Home

The Alienware x15 R2 is a powerful gaming laptop that offers a great balance of features and performance. It has a 15.6-inch QHD display with a 240Hz refresh rate, making it ideal for fast-paced games. The laptop is powered by an Intel Core i7-12700H processor and 32GB of RAM, ensuring that it can handle even the most demanding games. The Alienware x15 R2 also comes with a 1TB M.2 PCle NVMe SSD for fast storage.

The laptop also features a 1080p rear camera and a 720p front camera for video chatting. The Alienware x15 R2 is powered by a 87 Whr battery, which should provide you with several hours of gaming on a single charge. The laptop runs on Windows 11 Home.

Overall, the Alienware x15 R2 is a great option for anyone looking for a powerful gaming laptop with a great balance of features and performance.
