Nintendo Switch Specs

supertech Last Updated-April 15, 2023
Specification Nintendo Switch
Processor NVIDIA Custom Tegra processor
GPU Maxwell-based GPU
Memory 32 GB of internal storage
Display 6.2-inch 720p touchscreen
Rear camera 2 MP
Front camera 0.3 MP
Battery Up to 6.5 hours of gameplay
Operating system Nintendo Switch OS
Connectivity Wi-Fi 802.11ac, Bluetooth 4.2
Dimensions 9.4 x 4 x 0.55 inches (241 x 102 x 14 mm)
Weight 0.66 pounds (297 g)
Colors Neon Red, Neon Blue, Gray, Blue & Neon Yellow, White

The Nintendo Switch is a hybrid console that can be played both docked and undocked. It has a powerful processor and a beautiful display, making it a great choice for gamers of all ages. The Nintendo Switch is also very portable, making it perfect for taking on the go.
