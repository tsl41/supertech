Lenovo ThinkPad T16 Gen (AMD)

The Lenovo ThinkPad T16 Gen (AMD) is a powerful and versatile business laptop that is designed to meet the needs of today's professionals. It is powered by up to an AMD Ryzen™ 9 7980HX processor and AMD Radeon™ RX 6800M graphics, which provides the performance you need to handle demanding workloads. The ThinkPad T16 Gen (AMD) also features a 16-inch 4K (2560 x 1600) IPS display with Dolby Vision™, which delivers stunning visuals and immersive entertainment.

In addition to its powerful performance and stunning display, the ThinkPad T16 Gen (AMD) also features a number of other features that make it ideal for business users. These features include a spill-resistant keyboard, a fingerprint reader, and a variety of ports, including two Thunderbolt™ 4 ports, two USB-A ports, an HDMI port, and a microSD card reader.

The Lenovo ThinkPad T16 Gen (AMD) is a great choice for business users who are looking for a powerful and versatile laptop that can handle demanding workloads. It is also a great choice for users who are looking for a laptop with a stunning display and a number of security features.

Here are some of the key features of the Lenovo ThinkPad T16 Gen (AMD):

  • Up to an AMD Ryzen™ 9 7980HX processor
  • AMD Radeon™ RX 6800M graphics
  • 16-inch 4K (2560 x 1600) IPS display with Dolby Vision™
  • Spill-resistant keyboard
  • Fingerprint reader
  • Various ports, including two Thunderbolt™ 4 ports, two USB-A ports, an HDMI port, and a microSD card reader

The Lenovo ThinkPad T16 Gen (AMD) is available now starting at $1,599.
