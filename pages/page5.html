Specs for Lenovo Legion 5

supertech Last Updated-April 15, 2023
Specification Lenovo Legion 5
Display 15.6-inch Full HD IPS display
Processor AMD Ryzen 5000 Series or Intel Core i7
Graphics NVIDIA GeForce RTX 30 Series
Memory Up to 32GB DDR4 RAM
Storage Up to 1TB SSD
Keyboard Backlit keyboard with RGB lighting
Battery 80Whr
Operating system Windows 10 or Windows 11
Connectivity Wi-Fi 6, Bluetooth 5.1
Ports USB-C, USB-A, HDMI, Ethernet, Audio Jack
Weight Starting at 2.3 kg (5.07 lbs)
Colors Phantom Blue, Stingray White, Storm Grey

The Lenovo Legion 5 is a powerful gaming laptop that offers an immersive gaming experience with its high-performance components. It features a vibrant display, a powerful processor, and a dedicated graphics card. With ample memory and storage options, it can handle demanding games and multitasking with ease. The backlit keyboard with RGB lighting adds a stylish touch, and the large battery capacity ensures you can game for extended periods. The Lenovo Legion 5 is an excellent choice for gamers and power users looking for a reliable and performance-oriented laptop.
