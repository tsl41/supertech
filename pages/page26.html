HP EliteBook 860 G9 Notebook PC

The HP EliteBook 860 G9 Notebook PC is a powerful and versatile business laptop that is designed to meet the needs of today's professionals. It is powered by up to an 12th Gen Intel Core i9-12900H processor and Intel Iris Xe Graphics, which provides the performance you need to handle demanding workloads. The EliteBook 860 G9 also features a 16-inch 4K (2560 x 1600) IPS display with Dolby Vision™, which delivers stunning visuals and immersive entertainment.

In addition to its powerful performance and stunning display, the EliteBook 860 G9 also features a number of other features that make it ideal for business users. These features include:

  • A spill-resistant keyboard
  • A fingerprint reader
  • A variety of ports, including two Thunderbolt™ 4 ports, two USB-A ports, an HDMI port, and a microSD card reader
  • Security features such as a TPM chip, a fingerprint reader, and a Kensington lock slot
  • Up to 64GB of DDR5 memory
  • Up to 2TB of PCIe NVMe M.2 SSD storage
  • Long battery life of up to 15 hours
  • Durable and lightweight design
  • Available in a variety of colors

The HP EliteBook 860 G9 is a great choice for business users who are looking for a powerful and versatile laptop that can handle demanding workloads. It is also a great choice for users who are looking for a laptop with a stunning display and a number of security features.

Here are some additional details about the HP EliteBook 860 G9 Notebook PC:

  • The laptop is available in a variety of colors, including Natural Silver, Ash Silver, and Night Black.
  • The laptop has a starting weight of 4.1 pounds and a starting thickness of 0.7 inches.
  • The laptop has a battery life of up to 15 hours.
  • The laptop is MIL-STD 810G tested for durability.
