Dell Inspiron 14 Laptop

The Dell Inspiron 14 Laptop is a powerful and versatile laptop that is designed to meet the needs of today's users. It is powered by up to an 11th Gen Intel Core i7-1165G7 processor and Intel Iris Xe Graphics, which provides the performance you need to handle demanding workloads. The Inspiron 14 also features a 14-inch Full HD (1920 x 1080) IPS display with ComfortView Plus, which delivers stunning visuals and reduces eye strain.

In addition to its powerful performance and stunning display, the Inspiron 14 also features a number of other features that make it ideal for users. These features include:

  • A backlit keyboard
  • A fingerprint reader
  • A variety of ports, including two USB-A ports, an HDMI port, and a microSD card reader
  • Security features such as a TPM chip, a fingerprint reader, and a Kensington lock slot
  • Up to 16GB of DDR4 memory
  • Up to 512GB of PCIe NVMe M.2 SSD storage
  • Long battery life of up to 12 hours
  • Durable and lightweight design
  • Available in a variety of colors

The Dell Inspiron 14 is a great choice for users who are looking for a powerful and versatile laptop that can handle demanding workloads. It is also a great choice for users who are looking for a laptop with a stunning display and a number of security features.

Here are some additional details about the Dell Inspiron 14 Laptop:

  • The laptop is available in a variety of colors, including Platinum Silver, Alpine White, and Abyss Black.
  • The laptop has a starting weight of 3.7 pounds and a starting thickness of 0.6 inches.
  • The laptop has a battery life of up to 12 hours.
  • The laptop is MIL-STD 810G tested for durability.
