Galaxy Chromebook Go

The Galaxy Chromebook Go is a lightweight and portable Chromebook that is perfect for students and anyone who wants a device that is easy to take with them on the go. It has a sleek and stylish design, a long battery life, and a bright and vibrant display.

The Galaxy Chromebook Go is powered by an Intel Celeron N4500 processor and 4GB of RAM, which provides enough power for basic tasks like browsing the web, checking email, and streaming videos. It also has 64GB of storage, which is enough space to store your files and apps.

The Galaxy Chromebook Go has a 14-inch Full HD (1920 x 1080) display that is bright and vibrant. The display is also touch-screen, so you can interact with it using your fingers.

The Galaxy Chromebook Go has a long battery life of up to 12 hours, so you can use it all day without having to worry about running out of power. It also has a fast-charging feature that allows you to charge it up to 50% in just 30 minutes.

The Galaxy Chromebook Go is a great choice for students and anyone who wants a lightweight and portable Chromebook with a long battery life and a bright and vibrant display.

Here are some additional details about the Galaxy Chromebook Go:

  • Weight: 2.5 pounds
  • Dimensions: 12.6 x 8.7 x 0.5 inches
  • Display: 14-inch Full HD (1920 x 1080) touch-screen display
  • Processor: Intel Celeron N4500
  • RAM: 4GB
  • Storage: 64GB
  • Battery life: Up to 12 hours
  • Operating system: Chrome OS
  • Price: Starting at $349.99
