Xbox Series X Specs

Specification Value
CPU Custom Zen 2 CPU with 8 cores at 3.8 GHz (3.6 GHz with SMT)
GPU 12 TFLOPS, 52 CUs at 1.825 GHz
Memory 16 GB GDDR6

Optical drive 4K UHD Blu-ray drive
Display Up to 4K UHD at 120 Hz
Audio Dolby Atmos, DTS:X

Dimensions 11.85 x 5.95 x 3.01 inches

The Xbox Series X is the most powerful Xbox console ever made. It has a custom Zen 2 CPU with 8 cores at 3.8 GHz (3.6 GHz with SMT), a 12 TFLOPS RDNA 2 GPU, and 16 GB of GDDR6 memory. The Xbox Series X can play games at up to 4K UHD at 120 Hz, and it supports Dolby Atmos and DTS:X audio. The console also has a 4K UHD Blu-ray drive.
