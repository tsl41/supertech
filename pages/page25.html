HP EliteBook 830 G9 Notebook PC

The HP EliteBook 830 G9 Notebook PC is a powerful and versatile business laptop that is designed to meet the needs of today's professionals. It is powered by up to an 12th Gen Intel Core i9-12900H processor and Intel Iris Xe Graphics, which provides the performance you need to handle demanding workloads. The EliteBook 830 G9 also features a 16-inch 4K (2560 x 1600) IPS display with Dolby Vision™, which delivers stunning visuals and immersive entertainment.

In addition to its powerful performance and stunning display, the EliteBook 830 G9 also features a number of other features that make it ideal for business users. These features include a spill-resistant keyboard, a fingerprint reader, and a variety of ports, including two Thunderbolt™ 4 ports, two USB-A ports, an HDMI port, and a microSD card reader.

The HP EliteBook 830 G9 is a great choice for business users who are looking for a powerful and versatile laptop that can handle demanding workloads. It is also a great choice for users who are looking for a laptop with a stunning display and a number of security features.

Here are some of the key features of the HP EliteBook 830 G9 Notebook PC:

  • Up to an 12th Gen Intel Core i9-12900H processor
  • Intel Iris Xe Graphics
  • 16-inch 4K (2560 x 1600) IPS display with Dolby Vision™
  • Spill-resistant keyboard
  • Fingerprint reader
  • Various ports, including two Thunderbolt™ 4 ports, two USB-A ports, an HDMI port, and a microSD card reader
  • Security features such as a TPM chip, a fingerprint reader, and a Kensington lock slot

The HP EliteBook 830 G9 is available now starting at $1,799.
