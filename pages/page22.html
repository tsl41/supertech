AI Roadmap For Computer Science Student

technuel Last Updated-April 15, 2023

Artificial intelligence (AI) is a rapidly growing field with a wide range of applications. As a computer science student, you may be interested in learning more about AI and how you can use it in your career.

Here is a roadmap of AI for computer science students:

  1. Learn the basics of AI. This includes topics such as machine learning, natural language processing, and computer vision.
  2. Gain experience with AI tools and frameworks. There are many different AI tools and frameworks available, such as TensorFlow, PyTorch, and scikit-learn.
    3. Work on AI projects. This is a great way to learn more about AI and to apply what you have learned. There are many different AI projects that you can work on, such as building a chatbot, developing a recommendation system, or creating a self-driving car.
  3. Get involved in the AI community. There are many online and offline communities where you can connect with other AI enthusiasts and learn from them.
  4. Continue learning about AI. AI is a rapidly evolving field, so it is important to stay up-to-date on the latest developments.

By following this roadmap, you will be well on your way to becoming an AI expert.

  • Artificial Intelligence for Coders by Udacity
  • Introduction to Artificial Intelligence by edx
